# The Refarm Organization Graph

## Vision
The Refarm team does not just build a tool; we inhabit the system. Every member of the core team has their own sovereign account and their own graph. Collectively, we organize the "Refarm Graph" — the authoritative source of truth for the project's digital presence.

## How It Works
1. **Nodes as Organization Units**: Every feature, roadmap item, and architectural decision is a node in the graph.
2. **Collaborative Merging**: The project's public page is a "Materialized View" of a shared organization graph. When we sync our nodes, the page mold itself to our collective intent.
3. **Identity-Based Governance**: Contributions to the organization graph are signed by team members' keys, ensuring accountability and provenance.

## Implications
- **Self-Hosting**: We use our own primitives (Sync, Identity, Storage) to coordinate development.
- **Dynamic Identity**: If a team member leaves, their nodes remain, but their permission to update the "Organization Head" is revoked via the graph's governance rules.
- **Transparecy**: The public can browse the "Refarm Organization" directly as a graph of interconnected intents and commits.
